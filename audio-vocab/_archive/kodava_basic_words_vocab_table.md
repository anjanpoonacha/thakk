# Kodava Padipu — Basic Words Vocab Table

**Source:** Kodava Language! All basic words! Learn 250+ common words used in coorgi Language
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kh2OGQEMsdM
**Topic:** Comprehensive glossary of 250+ common Kodava Takk (Coorgi) words organized by theme

This document provides a structured vocabulary list from the Kodava language (Kodava Takk), also known as the Coorgi language. The words are sourced from the video and have been organized into thematic categories for easier learning. Romanization is kept to simple ASCII, and notes on morphemes or word usage are included where helpful.
