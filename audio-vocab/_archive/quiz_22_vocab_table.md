# Quiz 22 — Kodava Padipu Vocab Table

**Topic:** Kodava history, wedding traditions, geography, death customs, language vocabulary, ceremonial terms

## Kodava Historical and Cultural Knowledge

| English | Kodava Takk | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Year telephone was first introduced in Kodagu | Aayirathi ombayinuuti ambathadand | year 1880 |
| Time when bride-to-be is dressed in white bordered sari | Karika muripa dina vaiypath naarath | karika muripa = specific pre-wedding day; vaiypath = dawn; naarath = sunrise time |
| Which naad does Talakaveri belong to | Taavu naad | upper/source naad region |
| White canopy held over mourners in death ritual | Me-maaDi | me = above; maaDi = upper floor/canopy |

## Ritual Terms — Widow Rites
