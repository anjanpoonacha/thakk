## General Conversation

| English | Kodava Takk | Explanation |
|---|---|---|
| Do you (plural) know how many sessions we have done now? | Akka nanga ikka echchak sessions maaditthu gothappa ningakku? | akka = so, nanga = we, ikka = now, echchak = how many, maaditthu = have done, gothappa ningakku = do you (pl.) know? |
| Nine sessions are done, right? | Woyimbadh sessions aachi, ala? | woyimbadh = nine, aachi = became/are done, ala = right? |
| Are you (plural) able to understand Kodava takk well? | Ningakik Kodava takk chaayith' artha aayenuNda? | ningakik = for you (pl.), chaayith' = well, artha aayenuNda = are you understanding? |
| Shall we do a recap? | Oru recap maadana? | oru = one/a, maadana = shall we do? |
| How do you tell the time? | Gante noatu ennane? | gante = time/hour, noatu = to tell/see, ennane = how? |
