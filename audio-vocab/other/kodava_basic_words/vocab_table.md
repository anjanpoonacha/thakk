## Topics Covered

- Body parts (head, hand, eye, ear, nose, leg, teeth, hair, neck, stomach, tongue)
- Food and drinks (rice, milk, ghee, banana, mango, coconut, onion, chilli, curd)
- Nature (tree, forest, river, sea, hill, earth, sun, moon, star, water, air)
- Animals (dog, cat, bird, lion, pig, tiger)
- People and family (boy, kids, husband, wife, baby, brother, sister)
- Actions (come, go, dance, run, sit, stand, give, take, read, write, laugh, cry, sing)
- Descriptors (big, small, good, bad, fast, slow, hot, cold, bitter, sweet, new, old)
- Question words (what, who, whose, why, where, when, which, how)
- Pronouns (I, he, she, we, you, it, this, that, they)
- Time (day, night, morning, month, year, week, yesterday, tomorrow, minute)
- Common objects (book, glass, knife, paper, umbrella, vessel, window, blanket, sword)
- Concepts (love, truth, fear, health, language, money, family, wealth, question)
