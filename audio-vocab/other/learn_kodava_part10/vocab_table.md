# Learn Kodava Language Part 10 — Days of the Week — Kodava Vocabulary
> **Source:** days_of_week.mp3
> **Format:** Teaching video — days of the week in Kodava Takk with syllable-by-syllable pronunciation

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Sunday | naaLaacha | ನಾಳಾಚ | Kodava word for Sunday; lit. "day-day" (naaL = day, sun-day). |
| Monday | thingaLaacha | ತಿಂಗಳಾಚ | Kodava word for Monday; thingaL = moon/month. |
| Tuesday | chovvaacha | ಚೊವ್ವಾಚ | Kodava word for Tuesday; related to Chevva (Mars). |
| Wednesday | padhnaacha | ಪದ್ನಾಚ | Kodava word for Wednesday; related to Budha (Mercury). |
| Thursday | bealaacha | ಬ್ಯಾಳಾಚ | Kodava word for Thursday; related to Guru (Jupiter). |
| Friday | poLLiyaacha | ಪೊಳ್ಳಿಯಾಚ | Kodava word for Friday; poLLi = bright/auspicious (Shukra/Venus). |
| Saturday | cheniyaacha | ಚೆನಿಯಾಚ | Kodava word for Saturday; cheni = Shani (Saturn). |
| day | naaL | ನಾಳ್ | The basic word for 'day' in Kodava Takk. |
| first day | aadhya naaL | ಆದ್ಯ ನಾಳ್ | Phrase meaning 'the first day'; aadhya = first. |
| last day | kade naaL | ಕಡೆ ನಾಳ್ | Phrase meaning 'the last day'; kade = last/end. |
| then comes | pinne bappu | ಪಿನ್ನೆ ಬಪ್ಪು | Sequential connector: 'then comes' / 'next is'. |
| from there comes | allinjê bappu | ಅಲ್ಲಿಂಜೆ ಬಪ್ಪು | Sequential connector: 'from there comes'; allinjê = from there. |
| we say / they call it | aNNuva | ಅಣ್ಣುವ | Verb 'to say / to call'; used when naming things in Kodava. |
| Kodava learning | Kodava Padippu | ಕೊಡವ ಪಡಿಪ್ಪು | Name of the lesson series; padippu = learning/study. |
| team's / of the team | thandath'ra | ತಂಡತ್ರ | Genitive form of thanda (team); thandath'ra = of the team. |
| presentation | prastuthi | ಪ್ರಸ್ತುತಿ | Borrowed Sanskrit/Kannada word for 'presentation'. |
| introduction | parichaya | ಪರಿಚಯ | Borrowed Sanskrit/Kannada word for 'introduction'. |

## Topics Covered
- Days of the week (all seven)
- Sequential connectors (pinne bappu, allinjê bappu)
- Structural time vocabulary (naaL, aadhya, kade)
- Lesson framing vocabulary (Kodava Padippu, prastuthi, parichaya)
