# Kodava Padipu Quiz 1 — Kodava Culture & Festivals

Source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1ozH5UsgK20

| English | Kodava Takk | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| What is the popular chant while harvesting the ears of paddy during the festival of Putthari? | Poli poli deva | Putthari'randa kadu koyyoka entha eNdu koyyuva? |
| In a Kodava wedding, the mother blesses the bride or groom by gifting money. What is this ritual called? | OmbaNa / ponn paNa | mangala-kaara illengi mangala-karti aadhyavait paNa kettuvana — the first blessing with money at a wedding |
| What is the tenth day after Kaveri Sankramana called? | Pattalodi | Kaveri sankramana kainjitt paththane dina |
| What is a bed or mattress called in Kodava takk? | Kaduke | inanimate object loan word replacement |
| What does the word "julmaane" mean in Kodava takk? | danda | julmaane = punishment/fine |
| What is the dagger held by the Thirulakaara called? | kadtale | Thirulakaara = traditional weapon bearer |
| What is a temple (devasthana) called in Kodava takk? | Therike | idh Kodava takk'l entha eNdu eNNuwa? |
| During which zodiac sign is Kaveri Sankramana celebrated? | Thula raashi | Thula = Libra |
| What was the town of Madikeri originally called? | Muddu Raja Keri | Madikeri'ra moola pedha = original name of Madikeri |
| Which goddess is depicted in a thooku bolicha? | Lakshmi / Gaja Lakshmi | thooku bolicha = a decorative hanging panel |
