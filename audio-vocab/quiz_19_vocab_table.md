# Quiz 19 — Kodava Padipu Vocab Table

## Three Provinces of Kodagu — koDagira muund taleballya naaD

| English | Kodava Takk | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Northern province | maandale naaD | upper Kodagu region |
| Central province | kiggaTTu naaD | central Kodagu region |
| Southern province | eaLu naaD | lower/southern Kodagu region |
