# Quiz 20 — Kodava Padipu Vocab Table

**Topic:** Kannada words replaced by authentic Kodava Takk equivalents — vocabulary reclamation (Part 2)

## Kodava Takk Words — inda Kodava pada?

*Format: Kannada/common word → correct Kodava Takk word*

| Common Word (Kannada) | Kodava Takk | Explanation |
|----------------------|-------------|-------------|
| kaNNira reppe (eyelid) | kaNNira eambe | kaNNi = eye; eambe = lid/cover |
| aachaara (pickle) | aajaara | preserved/pickled condiment in Kodava |
| inaamu (reward/gift) | tommaane | gift or reward given in Kodava tradition |
| goade (wall) | keama | wall of a house in Kodava |
| bhaashe (language) | paaje | language/tongue; paaje is the Kodava word |
| vole (bangle) | adippu | bangle or bracelet |
| chappara (thatched roof/canopy) | pandapaNi | panda = canopy/shed; paNi = work/structure |
| kambiLi puLu (woolen blanket worm/caterpillar) | erpachch | woolly bear caterpillar |
| sambaLa (salary) | daavane | wages/salary in Kodava |
| muththayide (married woman with husband alive) | muttharchi | auspicious married woman status in Kodava |
