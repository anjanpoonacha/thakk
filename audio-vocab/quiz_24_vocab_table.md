# Quiz 24 — Kodava Vocabulary

> **Source:** Kodava Padipu — Kodava Quiz (cultural knowledge)
> **Format:** Question about Kodava culture/history → Kodava answer

| English | Kodava Takk | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Original language of the book Pattole Palame | kannada | First published in Kannada script |
| Number of types of weddings for a girl in Kodava custom | anjii | anjii = five |
| Original Kodava word for luck/fortune (adhrushta) | bhavisu | - |
| Body of water into which the Bara-pole from Kaggattu-nadu flows | arabi samudra | samudra = sea/ocean; arabi = Arabic/Arabian |
| Days of birth pollution period (pandethaa padha) | eeL | eeL = seven |
| Skeleton | muuLe guud | muuLe = bone; guud = cage/structure; lit. "bone cage" |
| Ritual when the bride lifts her veil | kaNNaNda mane'l niir edpa narathth | lit. "at the time of taking water in the groom's house" |
| Fog | puuLa poge | poge = smoke; lit. "flower smoke" or "misty smoke" |
| Term for brothers' wives (kinship) | maa thange | kinship term for wives of brothers in relation to each other |
| Place of origin of the Omkareshwara temple idol | kaashi | Kashi = Varanasi |
