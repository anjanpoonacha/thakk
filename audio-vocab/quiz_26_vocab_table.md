# Quiz 26 — Kodava Vocabulary

> **Source:** Kodava Padipu — Kodava Quiz (history and cultural trivia)
> **Format:** Question in Kodava → answer

| English | Kodava Takk | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Town on the border between Hunsur and Kodagu | Aane Choukoor | - |
| First person commemorated at the Gaddige in Madikeri | Mahadevammaaji | Gaddige = royal memorial/tomb complex in Madikeri |
| First organization to conduct the Keere Bali pole competition in Kodagu | Ponnoal Thanda | thanda = group/organization |
| Translators of Pattole Palame into Kodava language | Ajjini Kanda PramiLa pinya Mahesh Naachayya dampathiya | pinya = and; dampathiya = couple (husband and wife) |
| Large stones on the path from Bhagamandala to Talakaveri | Bhiima kallu | kallu = stone; named after the mythological Bhima |
| First Kodava to receive the Padma Shri award | Koadanda Rohini Poovayya | - |
| Year the Kadiyaara Kamba in Virajpet was erected | padhnaar January dandayirtha padhinanjii | January 14, 1925; padhnaar = 16; dandayirtha = twenty-one hundred (year); padhinanjii = 15 |
| Naadu where all four Makki Shaasthavu temples are located | Nooraambaad naadu | naadu = traditional region/division in Kodagu |
| First/oldest perige (clan subdivision) | Thavade perige | perige = subdivision of a Kodava okka (clan); aadhya = first |
| Kerala district connected to Brahmagiri hill besides Kodagu | Wainaad | Brahmagiri spans both Kodagu and Wayanad district |
