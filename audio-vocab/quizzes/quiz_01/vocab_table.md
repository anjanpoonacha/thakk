# Kodava Quiz 01 — Kodava Vocabulary
> **Source:** quiz_01.mp3
> **Format:** 10-question Kodava takk quiz covering festivals, rituals, objects, and places

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Kodava Study/Learning | Kodava Padippu | ಕೊಡವ ಪಡಿಪು | The name of the quiz program, meaning "Kodava Study". |
| Let there be abundance, O God | Poli poli Deva | ಪೊಲಿ ಪೊಲಿ ದೇವ | A harvest chant sung during the Puttari festival. |
| Harvest Festival | Puttari | ಪುತ್ತರಿ | The main harvest festival of the Kodava people. |
| Golden Offering (ritual) | Pombana | ಪೊಂಬಣ | Wedding ritual where the mother blesses the bride/groom with money. |
| Golden Offering (variant) | Ponn pana | ಪೊನ್ನ್ ಪಣ | A variant name for the Pombana wedding ritual. |
| Kaveri River Festival | Kaveri Sankramana | ಕಾವೇರಿ ಸಂಕ್ರಮಣ | Festival celebrating the annual rising of the river Kaveri's source. |
| Tenth Day | Pattalodi | ಪತ್ತಲೋದಿ | The tenth day after the Kaveri Sankramana festival. |
| Bed / Mattress | Kaduke | ಕಡುಕೆ | The Kodava word for a bed or mattress. |
| Fine / Penalty | Julmane | ಜುಲ್ಮಾನೆ | The Kodava word for a fine or penalty. |
| Ritual Role | Thirulakara | ತಿರುಳಕಾರ | A specific ritual role or person in Kodava tradition. |
| Dagger | Kadtale | ಕಡತಲೆ | A dagger, specifically the one held by the Thirulakara. |
| Temple | Thirike | ತಿರಿಕೆ | The Kodava word for a temple or 'devasthana'. |
| Libra (zodiac) | Tula rashi | ತುಲಾ ರಾಶಿ | The zodiac sign of Libra, during which Kaveri Sankramana occurs. |
| Muddu Raja's Town | Muddu Raja Keri | ಮುದ್ದು ರಾಜ ಕೇರಿ | The original name of the town of Madikeri. |
| Hanging Lamp | Thooku bolicha | ತೂಕು ಬೊಳಿಚ | A traditional Kodava hanging lamp, a sacred object. |
| Goddess Lakshmi with Elephants | Gajalakshmi | ಗಜಲಕ್ಷ್ಮಿ | The deity depicted in the Thooku bolicha lamp. |
