# Quiz 02 — Kodava Vocabulary
> **Source:** quiz_02.mp3
> **Format:** Visual quiz — 10 questions identifying Kodava objects, people, and cultural items

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Leech | UmbuLu | ಉಂಬುಳು | Blood-sucking worm found in damp areas of Kodagu. |
| Mulberry fruit | Chippuli paNN | ಚಿಪ್ಪುಲಿ ಪಣ್ಣ್ | Fruit of the mulberry tree; common in Kodagu gardens. |
| Rice strainer | Chibbi / kuu-chibbi | ಚಿಬ್ಬಿ / ಕೂ-ಚಿಬ್ಬಿ | Traditional vessel for draining starch from boiled rice. |
| Cape gooseberry | Gummatte paNN | ಗುಮ್ಮಟ್ಟೆ ಪಣ್ಣ್ | Small tangy fruit in a papery husk; also called ground cherry. |
| Coconut scraper | Palye kathi | ಪಲ್ಯೆ ಕತ್ತಿ | Traditional kitchen tool with serrated blade for grating coconut. |
| Woven mat | Palumbu | ಪಲುಂಬು | Traditional mat used at all auspicious and inauspicious Kodava occasions. |
| Sacred pillar | Kanni kamba | ಕನ್ನಿ ಕಂಬ | Main sacred pillar in the central hall of an Ainmane or Mundu mane. |
| Blow pipe | KoLape | ಕೊಳಪೆ | Bamboo or metal pipe used to blow air into a traditional wood-fired stove. |
| Women's bracelet | Paunchi | ಪೌಂಚಿ | Traditional solid gold bracelet worn in pairs by Kodava women. |
| Worm / Creature | puLu | ಪುಳು | General term for a worm, insect, or small creature. |
| Fruit | paNNu | ಪಣ್ಣು | General word for any type of fruit. |
| House | mane | ಮನೆ | General word for a house or home. |
| Kitchen | adige mane | ಅಡಿಗೆ ಮನೆ | Kitchen area; literally "cooking house". |
| Coconut | thenge | ತೆಂಗೆ | Coconut palm fruit; key ingredient in Kodava cuisine. |
| Stove / Hearth | ole | ಒಲೆ | Traditional wood-fired stove or hearth used for cooking. |
| Pillar | kamba | ಕಂಬ | General term for a pillar or post. |
| Ancestral home | Ainmane | ಐನ್ ಮನೆ | Ancestral home of a Kodava clan (okka); centre of cultural life. |
| Front house / Hall | Mundu mane | ಮುಂದು ಮನೆ | Front part or main hall of a traditional Kodava house. |
| Women | pommakka | ಪೊಮ್ಮಕ್ಕ | Plural term for women. |
| Hand | kai | ಕೈ | The word for hand. |
| Ornament / Jewellery | maale | ಮಾಲೆ | General term for an ornament, garland, or piece of jewellery. |
| Kodava Studies | Kodava Padippu | ಕೊಡವ ಪಡಿಪ್ಪು | Organisation name; literally "Kodava Learning / Kodava Studies". |
| What is it called? | enth'end annuva | ಎಂತಂದ್ ಅನ್ನುವ | Common quiz question phrase asking for the name of something. |
| Who? | daaru | ದಾರು | Interrogative pronoun meaning "who?". |

## Topics Covered
- Fauna (creatures found in Kodagu)
- Flora and food items
- Traditional household items and kitchenware
- Architecture and parts of a Kodava house
- People and body parts
- Cultural items and jewellery
- Interrogative phrases
