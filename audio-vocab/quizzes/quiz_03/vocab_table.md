# Professions Quiz — Kodava Vocabulary
> **Source:** data/thakk/source/audio/mp3/quiz_03.mp3
> **Format:** Quiz-style audio teaching Kodava names for 10 professions

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| let's learn | padikana | ಪಡಿಕನ | Verb meaning "let's learn" or "let's study". |
| profession | vrutti | ವೃತ್ತಿ | A loanword for profession or occupation. |
| different | boare | ಬೇರೆ | Adjective meaning "different" or "separate". |
| they call | kaakuva | ಕಾಕುವ | Verb meaning "they call" or "is called". |
| what | enthand | ಎಂತಂದ್ | Interrogative pronoun meaning "what". |
| we | nanga | ನಂಗ | First-person plural pronoun, "we". |
| or | illenge | ಇಲ್ಲೆಂಗೆ | Conjunction meaning "or", used to present alternatives. |
| Carpenter | Aachaari | ಆಚಾರಿ | A traditional carpenter or woodworker. |
| Cobbler | Moochi | ಮೋಚಿ | A person who mends shoes and works with leather. |
| Goldsmith | Thatta | ತಟ್ಟ | A traditional goldsmith or jeweler. |
| Butcher | Tharipava | ತರಿಪವ | Literally "one who cuts". Refers to a butcher. |
| Barber | Hajaama | ಹಜಾಮ | A barber, a role traditionally held by a specific community. |
| Washerman | MadivaaLa | ಮಡಿವಾಳ | A traditional washerman, part of the village service community. |
| Warrior | PadayaaLi | ಪಡಯಾಳಿ | A warrior or a member of an army. |
| Soldier | pataaLakaara | ಪಟಾಳಕಾರ | A soldier, often used in a more modern context. |
| Singer (male) | Paattukaara | ಪಾಟ್ಟುಕಾರ | A male singer. The suffix '-kaara' denotes a doer. |
| Singer (female) | paattukaarthi | ಪಾಟ್ಟುಕಾರ್ತಿ | A female singer. The suffix '-kaarthi' is the feminine form. |
| Blacksmith | Kolla | ಕೊಲ್ಲ | A traditional blacksmith or ironworker. |
| Watchman | Paarakaara | ಪಾರಕಾರ | A watchman or guard. From 'paara' (watch/guard duty). |

## Topics Covered
- Professions and occupations in Kodava takk
- Traditional craft workers
- Service roles
