# Quiz 04 — Kodava Vocabulary
> **Source:** quiz_04.mp3
> **Format:** 10-question Kodava takk quiz covering culture, food, geography, and notable personalities

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Stick game | Thaarakali | ತಾರಕಳಿ | A traditional Kodava stick game, similar to gilli-danda. |
| Stick game (alt. name) | Chinnithaar | ಚಿನ್ನಿತಾರ್ | Alternate name for the traditional Kodava stick game Thaarakali. |
| Language / Speech | takk | ತಕ್ಕ್ | The word for language or speech, as in 'Kodava takk'. |
| Game | kalik | ಕಳಿಕ್ | The general word for a game or play. |
| Job / Profession | Chaakri | ಚಾಕ್ರಿ | A word for service, job, or profession. |
| Bamboo basket lid | Thad'pe | ತಡ್ಪೆ | A flat bamboo lid on which newborns are traditionally placed for 12 days. |
| Gun flower | Thoaku puu | ತೋಕ್ ಪೂ | Flower used to worship weapons during the Kailpodh festival. |
| Gun | Thoaku | ತೋಕ್ | The Kodava word for a gun. |
| Flower | puu | ಪೂ | The Kodava word for a flower. |
| Broken-rice puttu | Paaputtu | ಪಾಪುಟ್ಟು | A steamed dish made from broken rice, a Kodava culinary staple. |
| House / Home | mane | ಮನೆ | The word for a house, home, or ancestral home (ainmane). |
| South Canara | Dakshina Kannada | ದಕ್ಷಿಣ ಕನ್ನಡ | A neighboring district of Kodagu. |
| Hassan | Haasan | ಹಾಸನ್ | A neighboring district of Kodagu. |
| Mysore | Mysuru | ಮೈಸೂರು | A neighboring district of Kodagu. |
| Elder's seat | Aimara | ಐಮರ | A built-in wooden seat reserved for the eldest member in the verandah. |
| Verandah | kaiyale | ಕೈಯಾಲೆ | The front, open verandah of a traditional Kodava ainmane (ancestral home). |
| C. B. Muthamma | C. B. Muthamma | ಸಿ. ಬಿ. ಮುತ್ತಮ್ಮ | The first female Indian Foreign Service (IFS) officer, from Kodagu. |
| Water heating vessel | Kalaa | ಕಲಾ | A large, traditional metal cauldron used for heating bath water. |
| Earlobe | Kemi thattu | ಕೆಮಿ ತಟ್ಟು | The lobe of the ear, literally 'ear flat part'. |
| Ear | Kemi | ಕೆಮಿ | The Kodava word for ear. |
| Flat part / Lobe | thattu | ತಟ್ಟು | A flat surface or part; used here to mean lobe. |
