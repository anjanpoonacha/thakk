# Quiz 05 — Kodava Vocabulary (Body Parts)
> **Source:** quiz_05.mp3
> **Format:** Quiz — body part names in Kodava Takk (10 questions)

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Education / Learning | padipu | ಪಡಿಪು | Kodava word for education or learning |
| Team's | thandathra | ತಂಡತ್ರ | Genitive of 'thanda' (team); 'of the team' |
| Presentation | prastuthi | ಪ್ರಸ್ತುತಿ | Loanword from Kannada/Sanskrit; presentation |
| Of the body | tadira | ತಡಿರ | Genitive of 'tadi' (body); 'of the body' |
| Body part / Limb | anga | ಅಂಗ | A part or limb of the body; loanword from Sanskrit |
| What | enthondu | ಎಂತೊಂದು | Interrogative pronoun meaning 'what' |
| Let us learn | padikana | ಪಡಿಕಣ | Hortative verb form; 'let us learn' |
| Eyebrows | kuyibu | ಕುಯಿಬು | Kodava word for eyebrows |
| Thumb | pebbattu | ಪೆಬ್ಬಟ್ಟು | Literally 'big finger'; pebb = big, attu = finger |
| Shoulder | nippu | ನಿಪ್ಪು | Kodava word for shoulder |
| Ankle | kaal'ra kard | ಕಾಲ್ರ ಕರ್ಡ್ | Literally 'foot's joint'; kaal = foot, kard = joint/knot |
| Uvula | kiru naavu | ಕಿರು ನಾವು | Literally 'small tongue'; kiru = small, naavu = tongue |
| Gums | oonu | ಊಣು | Kodava word for gums |
| Teeth | pallu | ಪಲ್ಲು | Kodava word for teeth |
| Eyelid | kannu'ra chatte | ಕಣ್ಣುರ ಚಟ್ಟೆ | Literally 'eye's cover'; kannu = eye, chatte = lid/cover |
| Eyelashes | kannu'ra reppe | ಕಣ್ಣುರ ರೆಪ್ಪೆ | Literally 'eye's lashes'; kannu = eye, reppe = lash |
| Chin | thaadi kuttu | ತಾಡಿ ಕುಟ್ಟು | Compound for chin; thaadi = beard area, kuttu = tip/point |
| Cheek | davade | ದವಡೆ | Kodava word for cheek |
| Neck | baale | ಬಾಳೆ | Kodava word for neck |
| Throat | thonde | ತೊಂಡೆ | Kodava word for throat |
| Waist | are | ಅರೆ | Kodava word for waist |

## Topics Covered
- Human body parts (head region: eyebrows, eyelid, eyelashes, chin, cheek, neck, throat, uvula, gums, teeth)
- Human body parts (torso and limbs: shoulder, thumb, ankle, waist)
- Kodava Takk grammar (genitive suffix -ra, hortative verb form -kana)
- Quiz framing vocabulary (enthondu, padikana, padipu)
