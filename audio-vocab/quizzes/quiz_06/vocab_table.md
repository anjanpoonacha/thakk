## Topics Covered
- Kinship terms: paternal and maternal relatives
- In-law terminology (maima, maami, momma, baava, machiNa)
- Possessive case suffixes (-anda, -ura, -ira, -yanda, -yara)
- Dative case (-akku, -ang'u)
- Question words and grammar particles (entha, aandu, illengi, enthandu)
- Okka (patrilineal clan) system terminology (manekara, ayya)
- Generational address terms (thaatha, kunjappa, balyappa)
