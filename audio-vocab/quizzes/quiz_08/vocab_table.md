# Quiz 08 — Kodava Vocabulary (Human Body Parts)
> **Source:** quiz_08.mp3
> **Format:** Quiz — Kodava names for human body parts (10 questions)

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Learning / Education | padippu | ಪಡಿಪ್ಪು | The act of learning or studying. |
| Language / Speech | thakk | ತಕ್ಕ | Refers to language or speech. |
| Body | thadira | ತಡಿರ | The physical structure of a person. |
| To the limb / part | angakku | ಅಂಗಕ್ | Dative case of 'anga' (limb/part). |
| Forehead | neththi | ನೆತ್ತಿ | The part of the face above the eyebrows. |
| Lips | thudi | ತುಡಿ | The two fleshy parts forming the mouth's edge. |
| Heel | mudi | ಮುಡಿ | The back part of the foot below the ankle. |
| Knee | kaalra charta | ಕಾಲ್ರ ಚಾರ್ಟ | The joint between the thigh and the lower leg. |
| Toe | mottu bera | ಮೊಟ್ಟು ಬೆರ | Any of the five digits at the end of the foot. |
| Intestine | karu | ಕರು | The lower part of the alimentary canal. |
| Skull | mandara oadu | ಮಂಡರ ಓಡು | The bony framework of the head; cranium. |
| Ring finger | moyira bera | ಮೊಯಿರ ಬೆರ | The finger next to the little finger. |
| Bones | moole | ಮೂಳೆ | The rigid parts of the skeleton. |
| Skin | thoalu | ತೋಲ್ | The thin layer of tissue forming the body's outer covering. |
