# Quiz 09 — Kodava Vocabulary (Parts of the Ainmane / Kodava House)
> **Source:** quiz_09.mp3
> **Format:** Vocabulary quiz — parts of a Kodava house (ainmane) in Kodava Takk

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Kodava learning | padippu | ಪಡಿಪು | The act of learning or studying. |
| Ancestral house | ainmane | ಐನ್ ಮನೆ | The traditional ancestral home of a Kodava family (okka). |
| Language | thakk | ತಕ್ಕ್ | Refers to the Kodava language. |
| House | aaroadha | ಆರೋಡ | A general term for a house or dwelling. |
| Back door | bembarapadi | ಬೆಂಬರ ಪಡಿ | The door at the back of the house. |
| Terrace | boalumadi | ಬೋಳ್ ಮಾಡಿ | An open terrace or flat roof area. |
| Bedroom | buddhavakaambare | ಬುದ್ಧವ ಕಾಂಬರೆ | A room for sleeping; a bedroom. |
| Bath shed / Bathroom | gudda | ಗುಡ್ಡ | Traditionally a separate shed for bathing; now refers to a bathroom. |
| Kitchen | umbalamane | ಉಂಬಳ ಮನೆ | The room where food is prepared; the kitchen. |
| Verandah | kaiyaala | ಕೈಯಾಲ | The open, roofed porch at the front of the house. |
| Courtyard | patti | ಪಟ್ಟಿ | The central open-to-sky courtyard within the ainmane. |
| Wooden door frame | kattoale | ಕಟ್ಟೋಳೆ | The frame of a doorway, often ornately carved in traditional houses. |
| Lane / pathway to house | oani | ಓಣಿ | The narrow path or lane leading to the house. |
| Bamboo gate | ubba | ಉಬ್ಬ | A simple gate, often made of bamboo, at the entrance of the property. |
