# Kodava Quiz 11 — Kodava Vocabulary
> **Source:** quiz_11.mp3
> **Format:** Quiz — 10 questions, each asking for three examples of a Kodava cultural/culinary/linguistic category

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Studies | padippu | ಪಡಿಪ್ಪು | Studies, learning, or education. |
| Presentation | prastuthi | ಪ್ರಸ್ತುತಿ | A presentation or offering. |
| Question | chodya | ಚೋದ್ಯ | A question in a quiz or inquiry. |
| Vessel | paathra | ಪಾತ್ರ | A general term for a vessel or utensil. |
| Flat pan | Saakala | ಸಾಕಲ | A flat pan or griddle, used for making dosas. |
| Ladle | chattuva | ಚಟ್ಟುವ | A ladle or serving spoon. |
| Cooking pot | thappale | ತಪ್ಪಲೆ | A general-purpose cooking pot. |
| Lamp | buLicha | ಬುಳಿಚ | A general term for a lamp. |
| Hanging lamp | ThookabuLicha | ತೂಕಬುಳಿಚ | A traditional hanging lamp, often made of brass. |
| Standing lamp | kuththubuLicha | ಕುತ್ತುಬುಳಿಚ | A traditional standing lamp, used in ceremonies. |
| Plate lamp | thaLiyathakki buLicha | ತಳಿಯತಕ್ಕಿ ಬುಳಿಚ | A lamp placed on a plate, central to Kodava rituals. |
| Pumpkin | KumbaLa | ಕುಂಬಳ | Pumpkin, a common vegetable in Kodava cuisine. |
| Beans | chettu | ಚೆಟ್ಟು | Beans, specifically referring to field beans (huruli). |
| Plantain | baine | ಬೈನೆ | Raw banana or plantain, used in cooking. |
| Song | paattu | ಪಾಟ್ಟು | A song. |
| Song of life | BaaLo paattu | ಬಾಳೋ ಪಾಟ್ಟು | A song narrating life events, sung during important ceremonies. |
| Thaali song | thaali paattu | ತಾಲಿ ಪಾಟ್ಟು | A song sung during the thaali (sacred necklace) tying ceremony. |
| Clothes song | batte paattu | ಬಟ್ಟೆ ಪಾಟ್ಟು | A song sung during the ritual of giving clothes to the bride. |
| Steamed dish | puttu | ಪುಟ್ಟು | A steamed dish, typically made from rice flour or broken rice. |
| Rice dumplings | Kadumbuttu | ಕಡುಂಬುಟ್ಟು | Steamed balls or dumplings made from broken rice. |
| String hoppers | nooputtu | ನೂಪುಟ್ಟು | Steamed rice noodles or string hoppers. |
| Milk rice cake | paaputtu | ಪಾಪುಟ್ಟು | A steamed rice cake made with milk, coconut, and cardamom. |
| Stone | kallu | ಕಲ್ಲು | A stone. |
| Mortar & pestle | KuttaNi / kuttu kallu | ಕುಟ್ಟಾಣಿ / ಕುಟ್ಟು ಕಲ್ಲು | A stone mortar and pestle used for pounding spices. |
| Grinding stone | ammi kallu / arpa kallu | ಅಮ್ಮಿ ಕಲ್ಲು / ಅರ್ಪ ಕಲ್ಲು | A flat stone with a roller, used for wet grinding masalas. |
| Splitting stone | kadichcha kallu / kadiva kallu | ಕಡಿಚ್ಚ ಕಲ್ಲು / ಕಡಿವ ಕಲ್ಲು | A stone used for splitting pulses or beans. |
| Mushroom | Kummu | ಕುಮ್ಮು | Wild mushroom, a prized monsoon delicacy. |
| Bamboo shoot | baimbaLe | ಬೈಂಬಳೆ | Tender bamboo shoot, harvested during the monsoon. |
| Cold soup | ThaLneer | ತಳ್ನೀರ್ | A cold soup/drink made with rice, curd, and water; a tambuli. |
| Cooked rice | KooLu | ಕೂಳ್ | Cooked rice, an ingredient for ThaLneer. |
| Curd | masikande | ಮಸಿಕಂಡೆ | Curd or yogurt. |
| Water | neeru | ನೀರ್ | Water. |
| Leafy greens | thoppu | ತೊಪ್ಪು | A general term for leafy greens or foliage. |
| Watercress-like green | KoLakke thoppu | ಕೊಳಕ್ಕೆ ತೊಪ್ಪು | A type of edible green found near water bodies. |
| A leafy green | thaathe thoppu | ತಾತೆ ತೊಪ್ಪು | A type of edible leafy green. |
| Fiddlehead fern | therme thoppu | ತೆರ್ಮೆ ತೊಪ್ಪು | An edible wild fern that grows in the monsoon. |
| Necklace | maale | ಮಾಲೆ | A necklace or garland. |
| Traditional necklace | Paththakku | ಪತ್ತಕ್ | A traditional necklace with a coin pendant, part of bridal wear. |
| Beaded necklace | jomaale | ಜೋಮಾಲೆ | A long necklace of grooved, coral-colored beads and gold. |
| Crescent pendant | kokkethaathi | ಕೊಕ್ಕೆತಾತಿ | A large, crescent-shaped pendant necklace worn by brides. |

## Topics Covered
- Quiz & Study Terminology
- Kitchenware (Vessels, Grinding Stones)
- Ritual Items (Lamps, Necklaces)
- Food & Ingredients (Vegetables, Rice Dishes, Greens, Monsoon Delicacies)
- Cultural Practices (Folk Songs)
