# Quiz 12 — Kodava Vocabulary (Clothing)
> **Source:** quiz_12.mp3
> **Format:** Vocabulary quiz — clothing items in Kodava takk

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Learning / studies | Padippu | ಪಡಿಪ್ಪು | The act of learning or studying. |
| Of the team | Thandathra | ತಂಡತ್ರ | Possessive form of "team". |
| Presentation | Prastuti | ಪ್ರಸ್ತುತಿ | A presentation or act of presenting. |
| What is this called in Kodava? | Idhunge Kodava thakkelu enth endu ennuva? | ಇದಿಂಗ್ ಕೊಡವ ತಕ್ಕಲ್ ಎಂತ್ ಎಂದು ಎಣ್ಣುವ? | Standard quiz phrase: "What is this called in the Kodava language?" |
| Shirt | Kameezu | ಕಮೀಜು | Upper-body garment; borrowed from Urdu/Hindi. |
| Pants | Suraalu | ಸುರಾಲು | Lower-body garment worn on the legs. |
| Handkerchief | Kai chouka | ಕೈ ಚೌಕ | Lit. "hand square"; small cloth for wiping face or hands. |
| Belt | Are patti | ಅರೆ ಪಟ್ಟಿ | Lit. "waist strip"; worn around the waist to hold clothing. |
| Monkey cap / balaclava | Moode thoppiya | ಮೂಡೆ ತೊಪ್ಪಿಯ | Cap covering head, ears, and neck. Lit. "head cap". |
| Mask / veil | Musku | ಮುಸ್ಕ್ | Cloth used to cover the face or head. |
| Frills of a saree | Peeli neri | ಪೀಲಿ ನೆರಿ | Decorative pleats at the border of a saree. |
| Stockings | Uddha kaal kousa | ಉದ್ದ ಕಾಲ್ ಕೌಸ | Lit. "long leg socks"; hosiery covering feet and legs. |
| Waistcoat / jacket | Kurtha | ಕುರ್ತ | Sleeveless or short upper-body garment worn over a shirt. |
| Saree | Podiya | ಪೊಡಿಯ | Traditional Kodava style of wearing a saree, pleats at the back. |
