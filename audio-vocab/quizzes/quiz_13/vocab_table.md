# Kodava House Parts — Kodava Vocabulary
> **Source:** quiz_13.mp3
> **Format:** Quiz — Kodava names for parts of a Kodava house (ainmane)

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Kodava Studies | Kodava Padippu | ಕೊಡವ ಪಡಿಪು | Study of Kodava language and culture. |
| House | mane | ಮನೆ | General term for a house or home. |
| Ancestral House | ainmane | ಐನ್ಮನೆ | Traditional ancestral home of a Kodava clan. |
| Let us learn | padikana | ಪಡಿಕನ | Common phrase used in educational contexts. |
| Kodava Language | Kodava takk | ಕೊಡವ ತಕ್ಕ್ | The language spoken by the Kodava people. |
| Steps / Stairs | mottu | ಮೊಟ್ಟು | Steps leading into or inside the house. |
| Drawing Room | alapa kombare | ಅಳಪ ಕೋಂಬರೆ | Room for entertaining guests; sitting room. |
| Main Hall | baade | ಬಾಡೆ | The central hall of the ainmane. |
| Well | kanathare | ಕಣತರೆ | A well; primary water source for the household. |
| Well (alternate) | kuuva | ಕೂವ | Alternate Kodava word for a well. |
| Attic / Loft | atta | ಅಟ್ಟ | Storage space under the roof. |
| Drying Yard | kala | ಕಳ | Open area for drying coffee, pepper, and paddy. |
| Courtyard Pole | boati | ಬೋಟಿ | Symbolic pole in the centre of the courtyard. |
| Sacred Lamp Area | nellakki | ನೆಲ್ಲಕ್ಕಿ | Sacred spot in the main hall with a hanging lamp; no separate pooja room in a Kodava house. |
| Verandah Platform | thithi | ತಿತ್ತಿ | Raised platform on the outer wall foundation. |
| Wardrobe / Closet | batte goodu | ಬಟ್ಟೆ ಗೂಡು | Place to store clothes; literally 'cloth nest'. |
