# Kodava Kitchen Utensils Quiz (quiz_14) — Kodava Vocabulary
> **Source:** quiz_14.mp3
> **Format:** Quiz — Kodava names for kitchen utensils, Q&A format

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Studies / Learning | Padippu | ಪಡಿಪು | Kodava word for studies or learning |
| Kitchen | umbala mane | ಉಂಬಳ ಮನೆ | Literally "eating house"; the Kodava kitchen |
| Utensil / Vessel | paatra | ಪಾತ್ರ | General term for a cooking vessel or utensil |
| Name | pedana | ಪೆದನ | The Kodava word for "name" |
| Large knife / Machete | Paleya katti | ಪಳೆಯ ಕತ್ತಿ | Used to scrape coconut or cut meat; paleya = old/traditional, katti = knife |
| Small oil pan | Enne cherku | ಎಣ್ಣೆ ಚರ್ಕ್ | Small pan for heating oil; enne = oil |
| String hopper press | Nooputtu vara | ನೂಪುಟ್ಟು ವರ | Press board for making nooputtu (string hoppers) |
| Ladle | Kai kane | ಕೈಕಣೆ | Stirring ladle; kai = hand, kane = spoon/ladle |
| Plate / Dish | Taliya | ತಳಿಯ | A plate or dish for serving food |
| Coconut shell ladle | Baa charate | ಬಾ ಚರಟೆ | Ladle made from a coconut shell; baa = coconut shell |
| Frying pan / Wok | Cheen chatti | ಚೀನ್ ಚಟ್ಟಿ | Pan for frying meat or eggs; cheen = Chinese, chatti = pan |
| Small tempering pan | Baal uruli | ಬಾಲುರುಳಿ | Small round pan for tempering (aggarane) curry |
| Rice gruel pot | Kanji kala | ಕಂಜಿ ಕಲ | Pot for storing leftover rice gruel (kanji) |
| Dosa griddle / Tawa | Dose kallu | ದೋಸೆ ಕಲ್ಲು | Flat griddle for making dosa; kallu = stone |

## Topics Covered
- Kitchen utensils (Kodava kitchen vocabulary)
- Food preparation tools and cookware
- Compound nouns: material + object (baa charate, dose kallu)
