# Quiz 15 — Kodava Vocabulary
> **Source:** quiz_15.mp3
> **Format:** Kodava Takk quiz — 10 questions, each asking for three examples from a category

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Study / Learning | padippu | ಪಡಿಪ್ಪು | Study, learning, or education. |
| Team | thanda | ತಂಡ | A team or group. |
| Presentation | prastuthi | ಪ್ರಸ್ತುತಿ | A presentation or offering. |
| Banana | baaLe | ಬಾಳೆ | General term for banana or plantain. |
| Small Banana | Puu baaLe | ಪೂ ಬಾಳೆ | A small, sweet variety of banana, literally 'flower banana'. |
| Plantain | mara baaLe | ಮರ ಬಾಳೆ | Plantain or cooking banana, literally 'tree banana'. |
| Dark-skinned Banana | kari baaLe | ಕರಿ ಬಾಳೆ | A variety of banana with a dark skin, literally 'black banana'. |
| Knife / Dagger | kathi | ಕತ್ತಿ | A general term for a knife or dagger. |
| Harvesting Knife | Koyi kathi | ಕೊಯಿ ಕತ್ತಿ | A knife used for harvesting, especially for cutting bunches of fruit. |
| Large Chopping Knife | odi kathi | ಒಡಿ ಕತ್ತಿ | A large, heavy knife for chopping or clearing brush. |
| Small Hand Knife | kai kathi | ಕೈ ಕತ್ತಿ | A small, general-purpose knife, literally 'hand knife'. |
| Housewarming | mane mangala | ಮನೆ ಮಂಗಳ | Housewarming ceremony. |
| Lamp | Chandooka | ಚಂದೂಕ | A traditional lamp, carried during ceremonies. |
| Cradle | thottu | ತೊಟ್ಟು | A cradle, symbolizing family continuity, carried during a housewarming. |
| Gun | thook | ತೂಕ್ | A gun, symbolizing the warrior heritage, carried during a housewarming. |
| Farm Work | aypaNi | ಆಯ್ಪಣಿ | Agricultural work in a farm or field. |
| Plough | Neangi | ನೇಂಗಿ | A plough, used for tilling the soil. |
| Yoke | noaga | ನೋಗ | A yoke, placed on oxen to pull a plough or cart. |
| Pickaxe / Hoe | guddali | ಗುದ್ದಲಿ | A pickaxe or hoe, a digging tool. |
| Measuring Unit | Maana | ಮಾನ | A traditional unit for measuring volume, especially grains. |
| Seer (unit of measure) | searu | ಸೇರು | A 'seer', a traditional unit of volume, roughly a litre. |
| Large Measuring Basket | pare | ಪರೆ | A large basket or container used for measuring grains in bulk. |
| Festival | namme | ನಮ್ಮೆ | A festival or celebration. |
| Festival of Arms | Kailpoadh | ಕೈಲ್ಪೋದ್ | Festival of arms, marking the end of the farming season. |
| Kaveri Festival | Kaveri SankramaNa | ಕಾವೇರಿ ಸಂಕ್ರಮಣ | Festival celebrating the birth of the river Kaveri. |
| Harvest Festival | Puthari | ಪುತರಿ | The harvest festival of Kodagu, celebrating the new rice crop. |
| Chair | Kurchi | ಕುರ್ಚಿ | A chair. |
| Cot / Bed | kattu | ಕಟ್ಟು | A cot or bed. |
| Table | meaji | ಮೇಜಿ | A table. |
| Chilli | maLu | ಮಳು | Chilli pepper. |
| Green Chilli | KaayimaLu | ಕಾಯಿಮಳು | Green chilli, literally 'unripe chilli'. |
| Capsicum / Bell Pepper | parangimaLu | ಪರಂಗಿಮಳು | Capsicum or bell pepper, literally 'foreign chilli'. |
| Round Chilli | boDDemaLu | ಬೊಡ್ಡೆಮಳು | A round, less spicy variety of chilli. |
| River | poLe | ಪೊಳೆ | River. |
| Kaveri (river) | Kaveri | ಕಾವೇರಿ | The sacred river Kaveri, which originates in Kodagu. |
| Kanike (river) | Kanike | ಕನಿಕೆ | A tributary river that joins the Kaveri. |
| Sujyothi (river) | Sujyothi | ಸುಜ್ಯೋತಿ | A mythical underground river believed to join the Kaveri and Kanike. |
| Bridegroom | mangalakaara | ಮಂಗಳಕಾರ | Bridegroom. |
| Ornament / Jewellery | chingaara | ಚಿಂಗಾರ | Ornament, jewellery, or decoration. |
| Sash | Chaale | ಚಾಲೆ | A red silk sash worn around the waist by a Kodava man. |
| Decorative Dagger | peechekathi | ಪೀಚೆಕತ್ತಿ | An ornamental dagger tucked into the waist sash (`chaale`). |
| Waist Ornament | thodang | ತೊಡಂಗ್ | A silver waist chain or ornament worn by the groom. |

## Topics Covered
- **Flora & Food:** Bananas, Chillies
- **Tools:** Knives, Farming Implements
- **Culture & Rituals:** Housewarming Ceremony, Festivals, Groom's Attire
- **Household:** Measuring Units, Furniture
- **Geography:** Rivers
