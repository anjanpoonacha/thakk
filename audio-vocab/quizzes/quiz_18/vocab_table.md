# Quiz 18 — Kodava Vocabulary
> **Source:** data/thakk/source/audio/mp3/quiz_18.mp3
> **Format:** Quiz — 10 questions asking for Kodava equivalents of Kannada/common words

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Learning, study | padipu | ಪಡಿಪು | The act of learning or studying. |
| Life | baaLe | ಬಾಳೆ | Refers to life or daily living. |
| Word | pada | ಪದ | A single distinct meaningful element of speech or writing. |
| Beautiful, good | chenna | ಚೆನ್ನ | Means beautiful, good, or nice. |
| Ginger | inji | ಇಂಜಿ | The rhizome of the ginger plant, used as a spice. |
| Anklet, ankle chain | kaachaara | ಕಾಚಾರ | An ornament worn around the ankle. |
| Nose stud | muukkodupu | ಮೂಕ್ಕೊಡುಪು | A piece of jewelry worn in a pierced nostril. |
| Jaggery | berchappa | ಬೆರ್ಚಪ್ಪ | A traditional non-centrifugal cane sugar. |
| Gilli-danda (game) | chinnithaaru / thaar kaLi | ಚಿಣ್ಣಿತಾರು / ತಾರ್ ಕಳಿ | A traditional game similar to tip-cat; also called thaar kaLi. |
| Waist | are | ಅರೆ | The part of the human body below the ribs. |
| Age | bayath | ಬಯತ್ | The length of time that a person has lived. |
| House | aaruuda | ಆರೂಡ | A building for human habitation. |
| Ivy Gourd | kaavakke | ಕಾವಕ್ಕೆ | A tropical vine, also known as tindora. |
| Weight, heavy | khana | ಖನ | The quality of being heavy. |
