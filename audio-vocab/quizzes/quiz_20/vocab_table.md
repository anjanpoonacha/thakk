# Quiz 20 — Kodava Vocabulary
> **Source:** quiz_20.mp3
> **Format:** Vocabulary quiz — 10 questions asking for Kodava equivalents of Kannada/common words

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| study / learning | Padippu | ಪಡಿಪ್ಪು | From 'padi' (to study/learn); name of the Kodava learning initiative. |
| team | thandathra | ತಂಡತ್ರ | A group or team. |
| let us learn | arinjona | ಅರಿಂಜೋನ | Hortative verb: "let's get to know / let's learn". |
| in daily life | baaLeel | ಬಾಳೀಲ್ | Locative of 'baaL' (life) — "in life / in daily life". |
| word | pada | ಪದ | A single meaningful unit of speech or writing. |
| eyelid | kaNNira embe | ಕಣ್ಣಿರ ಏಂಬೆ | Lit. "eye's lid"; kaNNira = of the eye, embe = eyelid. |
| custom / ritual / tradition | aajaara | ಆಜಾರ | Traditional practice or established custom. |
| reward / gift / prize | thommaane | ತೊಮ್ಮಾನೆ | Something given in recognition of service or achievement. |
| wall | keama | ಕೇಮ | An upright structure enclosing or dividing an area. |
| language | paaje | ಪಾಜೆ | The system of communication of a community. |
| stove / hearth / cooking fire | adippu | ಅಡಿಪ್ಪು | Traditional hearth or stove for cooking. |
| pandal / canopy / ceremonial shelter | pandapaNi | ಪಂದಪಣಿ | Temporary structure for shade, often erected for ceremonies. |
| caterpillar | erpachcha | ಎರ್ಪಚ್ಚ | Larval stage of a butterfly or moth; lit. woolly worm. |
| salary / wages | dhaavane | ಧಾವನೆ | Fixed regular payment from employer to employee. |
| married woman whose husband is alive | muttharchi | ಮುತ್ತರ್ಚಿ | Auspicious term (sumangali); a woman whose husband is living. |

## Topics Covered
- Household & Daily Life (hearth, wall, pandal)
- Cultural & Social Terms (custom, sumangali, reward)
- Body & Nature (eyelid, caterpillar)
- Language & Learning (language, word, study)
- Work & Economy (salary)
