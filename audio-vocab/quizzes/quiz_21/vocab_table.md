| Conducted | nadathina | ನಡತಿನ | Past tense of 'nadath' (to conduct, to run). |
| Family, clan | okka | ಒಕ್ಕ | The patrilineal Kodava clan or family name. |
| Which? | eadh? | ಏದ್? | Interrogative pronoun asking "which one?". |

| Towards | padi | ಪಡಿ | Preposition meaning towards or in the direction of. |
| Direction | dhikku | ದಿಕ್ಕು | Cardinal direction (e.g., North, South, East, West). |
| Is, exists | ippa | ಇಪ್ಪ | Verb 'to be' or 'to exist'. |
| East | Keakku | ಕೇಕು | The direction of the rising sun. |
| State, kingdom | raajya | ರಾಜ್ಯ | A political state or kingdom. |
| When | ekka | ಎಕ್ಕ | Interrogative adverb asking "at what time?". |
| Merged, joined | kuudiye | ಕೂಡಿಯೆ | Past tense of 'kuudu' (to join, to meet, to merge). |
| Year (A.D.) | isavi | ಇಸವಿ | Refers to a year in the Gregorian calendar. |
| Large/ancestral house | Ballemane | ಬಲ್ಲೆಮನೆ | A large, traditional house, often the ancestral home. |
| Original, old | muulla | ಮೂಲ್ಲ | Meaning original, ancient, or fundamental. |

| Language, speech | thakku | ತಕ್ಕು | A synonym for 'paaje', meaning language or talk. |
| Baby elephant | Kadichi | ಕಡಿಚಿ | The specific Kodava word for a baby elephant. |
| Four | naalu | ನಾಲ್ | The number four. |

| And, then | pinya | ಪಿಞ | Conjunction meaning 'and' or 'then'. |

| Collection, anthology | sangraha | ಸಂಗ್ರಹ | A collection or compilation of works. |
