| tradition / custom | paddhathi | ಪದ್ಧತಿ | A traditional practice or custom. |

| giving back the stone and tree | Kallu mara kaipo | ಕಲ್ಲು ಮರ ಕೈಪೋ | A ritual annulling a widow's rights, symbolizing returning her marital bond. |

| Tiger wedding ceremony | Nari mangala | ನರಿ ಮಂಗಲ | A ceremony that was traditionally held to celebrate a man's first tiger hunt. |

| child | kuNNi | ಕುಂಞಿ | A young child. |
