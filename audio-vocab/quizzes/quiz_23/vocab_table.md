# Kodava Quiz 23 — Insects & Bugs — Kodava Vocabulary
> **Source:** quiz_23.mp3
> **Format:** Quiz — 10 questions asking the Kodava Takk name for insects/bugs

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Education | paḍipu | ಪಡಿಪು | "Education" or "learning". |
| Team's | Tanḍathara | ತಂಡತರ | `tanda` (team) + `thara` (of the). "Of the team". |
| Presentation | prastuthi | ಪ್ರಸ್ತುತಿ | "Presentation". |
| What is this called in the Kodava language? | Idung Kodava paajel' enth'and' aṇṇuva? | ಇದಂಗ್ ಕೊಡವ ಪಾಜೆಲ್ ಎಂತಂದ್ ಅಣ್ಣುವ? | `idung` (to this) `paajel'` (in language) `enth'and'` (what) `aṇṇuva` (is said). |
| Firefly | Minnam puḷu | ಮಿನ್ನಂ ಪುಳು | `minnam` (lightning/flash) + `puḷu` (worm/bug). "Lightning bug". |
| Spider | Koalamba | ಕೋಲಂಬ | The Kodava word for spider. |
| Butterfly | Paapuli | ಪಾಪುಲಿ | The Kodava word for butterfly. |
| Snail | Koateanji / Koatanji | ಕೋಟೇಂಜಿ / ಕೋಟಂಜಿ | Two variants for snail, connected by `illengi` (or). |
| Grasshopper | Pacche pukka | ಪಚ್ಚೆ ಪುಕ್ಕ | `pacche` (green) + `pukka` (insect/hopper). "Green hopper". |
| Scorpion | Nyanḍrukka | ನ್ಯಂಡ್ರುಕ್ಕ | The Kodava word for scorpion. |
| Wasp | Kaḍundi | ಕಡುಂದಿ | The Kodava word for wasp. |
| Cockroach | Paathe | ಪಾತೆ | The Kodava word for cockroach. |
| Honeybee | Theanu oḷanji | ತೇನು ಒಳಂಜಿ | `theanu` (honey) + `oḷanji` (bee/fly). "Honey bee". |
| Fruit fly | Muuli | ಮೂಲಿ | General term for a fly, like a housefly or fruit fly. |

## Topics Covered
- Insects (firefly, butterfly, grasshopper, wasp, cockroach, honeybee, fruit fly)
- Arachnids (spider, scorpion)
- Mollusks (snail)
- Quiz question phrases
