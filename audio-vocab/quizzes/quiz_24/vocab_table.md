# Quiz 24 — Kodava Vocabulary
> **Source:** quiz_24.mp3
> **Format:** Kodava language quiz — 10 questions covering culture, geography, language, and customs

| English | Kodava Takk | Kannada Script | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Kodava learning | Kodava Padippu | ಕೊಡವ ಪಡಿಪು | The name of the program, meaning 'Kodava studies'. |
| Pattolapalame | Pattolapalame | ಪಟ್ಟೋಲೆಪಳಮೆ | A famous collection of Kodava folk songs and traditions. |
| Which | eadhu | ಏದು | Interrogative pronoun meaning 'which'. |
| Language | paajel | ಪಾಜೆಲ್ | The word for 'language' or 'speech'. |
| How many | echchaka | ಎಚ್ಚಕ | Interrogative word meaning 'how many' or 'how much'. |
| Wedding | mangalamuurtha | ಮಂಗಳಮೂರ್ತ | A type of wedding ceremony in Kodava custom. |
| What is it called | enth'endhu annuva | ಎಂತಂದ್ ಅಣ್ಣುವ | A common phrase asking 'what is it called?'. |
| Luck / Fortune | Bhavisu | ಭವಿಸು | The original Kodava word for 'adrishta' (luck). |
| Region / Land | naadu | ನಾಡು | A traditional region or land area in Kodagu. |
| Barapole | Barapole | ಬರಪೊಳೆ | Name of a major river originating in Kodagu. |
| Where | yelli | ಎಲ್ಲಿ | Interrogative adverb meaning 'where'. |
| For the one who gave birth | peththavakkuk | ಪೆತ್ತವಕ್ಕಕ್ | Refers to the mother after childbirth. |
| Birth pollution | pole | ಪೊಲೆ | A period of ritual impurity after childbirth or death. |
| Skeleton | MooLe guudu | ಮೂಳೆ ಗೂಡು | The Kodava term for skeleton, literally 'bone cage'. |
| Veil | muskuna | ಮುಸ್ಕುನ | The veil worn by a Kodava bride. |
| When | ekka | ಎಕ್ಕ | Interrogative adverb meaning 'when'. |
| Fog | PooLa poge | ಪೂಳ ಪೊಗೆ | The Kodava term for fog or mist. |
| Wives / Women | ponnaaLakku | ಪೊಣ್ಣಾಳಕ್ | Plural form for women or wives. |
| Brothers' wives | Maa thange | ಮಾ ತಂಗೆ | Kinship term for the wives of one's brothers. |
| From where | ellinj | ಎಲ್ಲಿಂಜ್ | Interrogative, 'from where'. |
| Was it brought | edutha bandhiye | ಎಡುತ ಬಂದಿಯೇ | Phrase asking about the origin of an object. |
| And / Then | pinne | ಪಿನ್ನೆ | Conjunction meaning 'and then' or 'also'. |
| Thank you | Dhanyavaada | ಧನ್ಯವಾದ | A formal way to say 'thank you', a loanword. |
