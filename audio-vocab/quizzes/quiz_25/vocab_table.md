## Topics Covered
- Child ceremonies (kanni mangala, kemi kuthi mangala, kuthi'k nippadhê)
- Traditional jewelry (kaira and its three parts: pebbattu, uyi, bera)
- Clan system (okka, Talakaveri clans: Mandira, Manavattira, Pattamada)
- Ancestral home (aimara / mund mane, carved pillar imagery)
- Puttari harvest festival foods (kalnji, thambuttu, akki payasa)
- Kodava dialects (naadu thakk, desha thakk, uuru thakk)
- Jewelry metals (ponnê, boLLi, pichchoaLe)
- Historical names for Kodagu (Kodavu, Kroodadesha, Kodumala)
- Folk dance implements (chauri, joppe kole, kathi)
- Kaveri river tributaries in Tamil Nadu (Bhavani, Noyyal, Amaravati)
