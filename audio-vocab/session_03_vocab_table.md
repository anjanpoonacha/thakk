## Greetings

| English | Kodava Takk | Explanation |
|---|---|---|
| How are you doing? | niin ennane uLLiya? | niin = you (singular), ennane = how, uLLiya = are |
| I am doing well. | naan chaayi'l uLL. | naan = I, chaayi'l = well, uLL = am |
| How are you all? | ninga ennane uLLira? | ninga = you (plural), ennane = how, uLLira = are |
| We are doing well. | nanga chaayi'l uNd. | nanga = we, chaayi'l = well, uNd = are |
