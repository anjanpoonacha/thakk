## Topics Covered
- Pronouns (Personal, Demonstrative, Interrogative)
- Numbers (20-40)
- Emotions and States of Being (happy, sad, angry, well)
- Colors
- Family and Relationships
- Household Items and Objects
- Food Items (fruits, vegetables, staples)
- Basic Verbs and Sentence Structure
- Possessives and Location (prepositional cases)
