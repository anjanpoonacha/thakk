## Topics Covered
- Numbers 41–50 (naapath series + aimbadu for 50)
- Animals and birds (pakshi, kaake, koli, paambu, umblu, chitte, pulu)
- Paternal family relationships (bendu chachan — thatha, avayya, balyappa, bojappa, kunyappa, maami, mama, bava, mamma)
- Household items (batte, podiya, sural, chalemundu, kambli, cheepu, kannadi, chombu, bindige, gante, mukkali, palya)
- Food (otti, pajji, mor pajji, noo puttu, bellath neeru, paal puttu, koli kari, kadumbuttu, pandi kari, ney kulu, kori erchi, koli barthadu)
- Positional words (adiyilu, kodiyilu, porme, olulu, minyattilu, bayyattilu, baltabarilu, eduttabarilu)
- Compound verbs with maaduwa (kelsa, adige, shuru, bejaara, jaasti, kammi, duddu)
- Grammar: kamba (to see), -ku suffix (dative), sentence patterns with yedu/yelli/undu
