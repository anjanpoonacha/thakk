- Numbers (91-100)
- Time expressions (quarter, half, three-quarters)
- Seasons (summer, monsoon, winter)
- The Puttari festival (customs, food, specific vocabulary)
- Verbs (infinitives, commands, and conjugations)
- Past tense (simple past and past continuous)
- Pronouns and their cases (dative, possessive)
- Comparative adjectives
