**Kaad**. We all know **kaad**, right? **Kaad** is forest.

**Belu**. **Bel pani'k pokana**. What is that?
**Belu**. **Belu** is field. **Bel pani** is field work. Work that happens specifically on the paddy field is called **bel pani**.

**Bel'l nellu boLthuva. Entha boLthu bel'l? Nellu. Nellu** is paddy. Paddy is grown in the paddy field.

Now, let's go through some household verbs.

**Kathuw'k**.
to wash, especially dishes. **Kathuw'k**. **kai kathuw'k**, **paathra kathuw'k**.

**Volip'k**.
Only for clothes, you say **volipa**.
You don't say **batte katho**. You say **batte volipa**.
But **paathra katho**. Okay? **kai katho**, **paathra katho**.
Everything to wash is **katho**. But only for clothes it is **volipa**.

**Vodukkuw'k**. To sweep the house is called **vodukkuw'k**.

**Madukkuw'k** is to fold the clothes.

**Neer bookuw'k**. to pour. to pour water, **neer bookuw'k**.

**Poo koyyuda. Koyyuw'k.** to pluck flowers, **poo koyyuw'k**.

**Adige maaduw'k**. to cook, to make food, **adige maaduw'k**.
