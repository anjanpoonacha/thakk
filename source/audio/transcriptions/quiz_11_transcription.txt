**Kodava paDipu**. **daNDathara prastuthi Kodava** Quiz.

Team **Kodava paDipu** presents **Kodava** Quiz.

**muundaaNda choadhu**.

**umbaLa mane'l upayogichuDo eadhengi muundh tharathra paathra**.
Name any three forms of kitchen ware used in a **Kodava** kitchen.
**saakala**, **chaTTuva**, **thappale**.

**Kodava mane'l illengi nammel upayogichuDo eadhengi muundh tharathra boLucha**.
Name any three forms of lamps used in a **Kodava** household or during **Kodava** rituals.
**thuuku boLucha**, **kuththu boLucha**, **thaLiyathakki boLucha**.

**Kodava thakk'l eadhengi muundh tharkari'ra pedha**.
Name any three vegetables in **Kodava thakk**.
**kumbaLa**, **cheTTu**, **bayne**.

**Kodava paaDuvanth eadhengi muundh janapada paaTTu**.
Name any three forms of folk songs sung by **Kodavas**.
**baaLo paaTTu**, **thaali paaTTu**, **baTTe paaTTu**.

**Kodava mane'l thari'l maaduvantha eadhengi muundh puTTu**.
Name any three types of dishes which are made from broken rice.
**kaDumbuTTu**, **nuu puTTu**, **paa puTTu**.

**Kodava mane'l aDige maaduvaaka upayogichuDuva eadhengi muundh kallu**.
Name any three forms of stone grinders used in a **Kodava** kitchen.
**koTTaaNi illengi kuTTu kallu**, **ammi kallu illengi arpa kallu**, **kaDicha kallu illengi kaDiya kallu**.

**naisargikavaayiththu boLiyuvanth idu maLegaalathilu illengi maLe pojjokka maathra puTTuvu**.
Name any three forms of crops that are grown only during monsoon or those grown when it rains.
**kummu**, **baymbaLe**, **therme thoppu**.

**thaLNeeru maaDoka enthane kuuTithu maaduva**.
Name the three things used while making **thaLNeeru**.
**kuuLu**, **masikaNDe**, **neeru**.

**kere eeri'l illengi thooDu'ra bari'l boLiyuvanth muundh tharathara thoppu**.
Name any three types of greens grown by the lake side or near the stream.
**koLakke thoppu**, **thaathe thoppu**, **therme thoppu**.

**Kodava mangalath mangalakarthi boaLe'k iDuva eadhengi muundh tharathara maalu**.
Name any three jewelry worn by a **Kodava** bride around her neck.
**paththakku**, **joamaale**, **kokke thaathi**.
