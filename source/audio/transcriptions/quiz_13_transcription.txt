**Kodava padipu**. **Thandathara** **prastuti** Kodava Quest.

Team **Kodava padipu** presents Kodava Quest.

**Ii** video'l **nanga Kodag'ra mane illengi ain mane'k sambandapatta jaaga'k Kodava takk'l enth'endh annuva'ndh padikana.**

In this quiz, you will learn Kodava names of various parts of a **ain mane** or a Kodava house in Kodagu. We request you to use them regularly.

**Bhaaga dhandu**.

Steps. **Idhungu Kodava takk'l enth'endh annuva.**

**Mottu**.

Drawing room or sitting room. **Idhungu Kodava takk'l enth'endh annuva.**

**Alupa koambare**.

Main hall. **Idhungu Kodava takk'l enth'endh annuva.**

**Baada**.

Well. **Idhungu Kodava takk'l enth'endh annuva.**

**Kanatthara illengi kuuva**.

A story in the upper part of the house, below the roof. **Idhungu Kodava takk'l enth'endh annuva.**

**Atta**.

**BoLuthura boLena oNangichudo kuLLo jaaga.** Drying yard. **Idhungu Kodava takk'l enth'endh annuva.**

**kaLa**.

**Patti'l ippa oar kamba.** A pole in the central part of the courtyard. **Idhungu Kodava takk'l enth'endh annuva.**

**BoaTi**.

**dhaavaLa thudippo illengi booduvo kuLLo jaaga.** Place of worship. **Idhungu Kodava takk'l enth'endh annuva.**

**nellakki**.

**Kodava mane'l pooje koambare annuva baare jaaga ille.**

**mane'thra porme gaaDe'k kuuduna aDipaayathra oar bhaaga.** Extended platform of the foundation from the outer wall of the building. **Idhungu Kodava takk'l enth'endh annuva.**

**thiththi**.

**Batte beppakuLLo jaaga.** A wardrobe. **Idhungu Kodava takk'l enth'endh annuva.**
