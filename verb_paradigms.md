# Kodava Takk — Verb Paradigms

Full conjugation tables for Kodava verbs across all persons and tenses.
Each verb section gives: nonpast · past · present progressive · past progressive.

Confidence key:
- ✅ textbook-verified
- 🟡 textbook-sourced (pattern may vary by speaker)

## ipp'k — to be (auxiliary)

**Progressive particle**: injand

The verb ipp'k is the backbone of all progressive and perfect tenses.
Its forms are required to construct compound tenses for every other verb.

### Nonpast (present / habitual)

The nonpast forms of ipp'k (to be). Used for temporary states: location, emotion, existence.

| Person | Kodava | Kannada | Notes |
|--------|--------|---------|-------|
| naan (I) | uLL | ಉಳ್ | ✅ |
| niin (you sg.) | uLLiya | ಉಳ್ಳಿಯ | ✅ |
| āāwu (he/she/it) | uNd | ಉಂಡ್ | ✅ |
| nanga (we) | uNd | ಉಂಡ್ | ✅ |
| ninga (you pl./formal) | uLLira | ಉಳ್ಳಿರ | ✅ |
| ainga (they) | uNd | ಉಂಡ್ | ✅ |

Negative: ille · Interrogative: uNdaa?

### Future

The future forms of ipp'k (to be). Also used as nonpast endings for all regular -uw'k verbs.

| Person | Kodava | Kannada | Notes |
|--------|--------|---------|-------|
| naan (I) | ippii | ಇಪ್ಪಿ | ✅ |
| niin (you sg.) | ippiiya | ಇಪ್ಪಿಯ | ✅ |
| āāwu (he/she/it) | ippa | ಇಪ್ಪ | ✅ |
| nanga (we) | ippa | ಇಪ್ಪ | ✅ |
| ninga (you pl./formal) | ippiira | ಇಪ್ಪಿರ | ✅ |
| ainga (they) | ippa | ಇಪ್ಪ | ✅ |

Negative: ipp'le · Interrogative: ippaa?

### Past

The past forms of ipp'k (to be). Used to build past progressive and past perfect tenses.
